Explore Maldives

Top 5 Tourist Places

Male City

Capital of Maldives, a bustling island city with mosques, markets, and modern infrastructure. Home to the Grand Friday Mosque and vibrant local markets. Best time: November-April (dry season). Entry free. Activities: Visit mosques, explore markets, cultural tours.

Maafushi Island

Popular local island offering authentic Maldivian culture and pristine beaches. Known for its laid-back vibe and community feel. Best time: December-April. Entry free. Activities: Snorkeling, bike tours, local cuisine, beach relaxation.

Banana Reef

Famous diving and snorkeling spot with vibrant coral gardens and marine life. Part of the UNESCO biosphere reserve. Best time: November-April. Diving entry: ~$50. Activities: Scuba diving, snorkeling, marine life observation.

Alimatha Island

Relaxing beach destination with white sand beaches and turquoise waters. Perfect for unwinding and water activities. Best time: December-March. Entry free. Activities: Beach lounging, water sports, sunset cruises.

Sun Island

Luxury resort island with overwater bungalows and world-class amenities. Offers privacy and indulgence. Best time: November-April. Entry free. Activities: Spa treatments, water sports, fine dining, private beach access.
