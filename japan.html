Explore Japan

Top 5 Tourist Places

Tokyo

Modern metropolis blending cutting-edge technology with traditional culture. Home to Shibuya Crossing and Tsukiji Market. Best time: March-May or September-November. Entry free, JR Pass for transport. Activities: shopping in Harajuku, visiting Senso-ji Temple, exploring Akihabara.

Kyoto

Ancient capital with over 1,600 temples, including Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion). Known for geisha districts and bamboo forests. Best time: March-April (cherry blossoms) or October-November. Temple entry: ¥400-1000. Activities: tea ceremonies, Zen gardens, traditional ryokan stays.

Mount Fuji

Iconic dormant volcano and UNESCO site, Japan's highest peak at 3,776m. Offers hiking trails and scenic views. Best time: July-September for climbing. Climbing permit: ¥1,000-5,000. Activities: hiking, hot springs, Lake Kawaguchi cruises.

Osaka

Vibrant city famous for street food, nightlife, and Universal Studios. Gateway to Kansai region. Best time: March-May or September-October. Entry free, subway day pass ¥600. Activities: Dotonbori food tours, Osaka Castle, aquarium visits.

Nara

Historic city with Todai-ji Temple housing the world's largest bronze Buddha. Famous for tame wild deer in Nara Park. Best time: October-November. Temple entry: ¥800. Activities: deer feeding, temple tours, traditional crafts.
