Explore Paris

Top 5 Tourist Places

Eiffel Tower

Iconic symbol of Paris, built in 1889 for the World's Fair. Offers panoramic views from three levels. Best visited at sunset. Entry: €16-28 depending on level. Open daily, last entry 11 PM in summer.

Louvre Museum

World's largest art museum, home to the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo. Originally a palace, now houses 35,000 works. Best time: early morning or late afternoon. Entry: €17, free for under 18s. Open 9 AM-6 PM, closed Tuesdays.

Notre Dame

Historic Gothic cathedral damaged by fire in 2019, currently under restoration. Features stunning stained glass and architecture. Free entry to exterior, interior closed until 2024. Best viewed from Île de la Cité.

Seine River Cruise

Romantic river experience with views of major landmarks. Cruises last 1-2 hours, narrated in multiple languages. Best at sunset. Tickets: €15-20. Departs from Pont Neuf, runs April-October.

Montmartre

Artistic & cultural hub, home to Sacré-Cœur Basilica and Moulin Rouge. Known for street artists and cafes. Free to explore, Basilica entry free. Best time: daytime for views, evening for shows.
