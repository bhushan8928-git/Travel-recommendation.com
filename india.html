Top 5 Tourist Places

Taj Mahal

Symbol of love in Agra. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this ivory-white marble mausoleum was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. Best visited at sunrise or sunset. Entry fee: ₹50 for Indians, ₹1100 for foreigners.

Goa

Beaches and nightlife. Known for its beautiful beaches, Portuguese architecture, and vibrant nightlife. Popular spots include Calangute and Baga beaches. Best time to visit: November to May. Famous for seafood and water sports.

Kerala

Backwaters & nature. Famous for its serene backwaters, houseboat cruises, and Ayurvedic treatments. Alleppey and Kumarakom are top destinations. Best time: June to September (monsoon for backwaters). Known as 'God's Own Country'.

Jaipur

Royal palaces & forts. The Pink City features magnificent palaces like City Palace and Amber Fort. Known for its rich history and architecture. Best time: October to March. Don't miss the Hawa Mahal and Jantar Mantar.

Ladakh

Mountains & adventure. High-altitude desert with stunning landscapes, monasteries, and adventure activities like trekking and biking. Leh is the main town. Best time: June to September. Requires acclimatization due to altitude.
