Explore Dubai

Top 5 Tourist Places

Burj Khalifa

Tallest building in the world at 828 meters. Offers stunning views from the observation decks. Best visited at sunset. Entry to At the Top: AED 125-400 depending on level. Open daily from 8 AM to 1 AM.

Dubai Mall

Luxury shopping destination with over 1200 stores, including brands like Louis Vuitton and Gucci. Features Dubai Aquarium, VR Park, and ice skating rink. Connected to Burj Khalifa. Free entry, shops open 10 AM to 10 PM.

Palm Jumeirah

Artificial island shaped like a palm tree, home to luxury resorts and villas. Features the iconic Atlantis The Palm hotel. Best explored by monorail or boat. Entry to public areas is free, but attractions have fees.

Desert Safari

Adventure in the desert with dune bashing, camel riding, and traditional Bedouin camps. Includes BBQ dinner and belly dancing. Best time: November to April. Tours cost AED 200-400, usually 3-4 hours.

Dubai Marina

Modern waterfront area with skyscrapers, yachts, and luxury apartments. Features JBR Beach, walking paths, and dining options. Popular for sunset views and water activities. Free to explore, restaurants open late.
